In this task you create pictures by selecting and/or deselecting symbols presented in a row across {{ num_pictures }} columns (see screenshot below). There are {{ num_pictures }} different symbols and each row of symbols represents a picture. An alien from outer space likes to buy such pictures and it will pay different amounts for different pictures with different symbols, but you don’t know what the alien likes and how the interconnectedness between the different symbols influences the payoff. A first example picture and its payoff are already displayed.

You create every picture jointly with three other participants who you are randomly allocated to. One of you is P1, the other ones are P2, P3, and P4. All of you are allocated to one area with three symbols that you can select or deselect. P1 is responsible for the three symbols on the left, P2 is responsible for the three symbols on the middle-left, P3 is responsible for the three symbols on the middle-right, and P4 is responsible for the three symbols on the right. All of you can freely change any symbol in your area, but you cannot make changes to symbols in the other participant’s area. In every round, you can change as many symbols as you wish (0-3), but you must make a decision for every symbol in every round as the alien considers for the evaluation of the picture the entire combination from all areas.

Before you change any symbols, you must chat with the other participants. When all of you decide to end the chat, and click on the corresponding button, you and the other participants will be able to change the symbols in your area. When you’re ready with your choice of selected and not-selected buttons in your area, you can click on the “Submit” button. After your and the other participants’ choices are submitted, the ‘P1 Payoff’, ‘P2 Payoff’, ‘P3 Payoff’, and the ‘P4 Payoff’ will then be shown in addition to the ‘Joint Payoff’, which is the accumulation of both individual payoffs. With the display of these payoffs, the round is completed, and you can continue with the chat for the next round.

While you are not able to return to prior rounds, the alien is willing to buy the same combination more than once; the payoff will then stay the same. Also note that you and the other participants have the exact same information, i.e. you see all selected and not-selected symbols and payoffs in all areas in all prior rounds. After submitting 10 pictures, the alien buys all pictures you jointly created and pays all of you the accumulated price ‘Joint Payoff’. The value of the pictures will change when you move to the next alien who will purchase the next block of {{ num_pictures }} pictures.

For this task, you will earn a bonus of 2 pence (£0.02) per 10 points.
